Italian Beef That Will Rock Your Socks

The Italian Beef Sandwich is a classic Chicago-style delicacy. It consists of thinly sliced, juicy roast beef cooked with Italian spices and served on an Italian roll. The beef is often slow-roasted and thinly sliced to maximize tenderness and flavor. The sandwich is typically dipped or "wet" in flavorful beef au jus, adding even more succulence to each bite. To enhance the taste, you can customize your sandwich with sweet or hot peppers, sautéed onions, and melted cheese. The Italian Beef Sandwich is a must-try for meat lovers visiting Chicago!

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced roast beef
  • Italian spices
  • Italian roll
  • Beef au jus
  • Sweet or hot peppers
  • Sautéed onions
  • Melted cheese

Step By Step Directions

  1. Preheat the beef au jus in a saucepan.
  2. Thinly slice the Italian roll.
  3. Dip the sliced roast beef into the warm au jus to moisten.
  4. Place the moistened roast beef on the Italian roll.
  5. Add your desired amount of Italian spices.
  6. Add sautéed onions and sweet or hot peppers.
  7. Melt cheese on top of the sandwich if desired.
  8. Serve with a side of beef au jus for dipping.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Italian Beef Sandwich!
